About

Full Stack Developer & Experienced Non-profit Arts Location Manager with extensive background in Psychology and Visual Art & Design. I am a perpetual learning machine that lives at the intersection between science and the arts. I have always been drawn to finding solutions to streamline processes and make work more efficient. I am generally the "go-to" team member for technology and software questions and throughout various positions I have often been chosen to train or lead. I became interested in learning code and software development over the last few years while managing a non-profit arts studio. As a location manager it is essential to be as efficient as possible. When I began creating a website with Google Sites that was to serve as a communication hub for teaching artists and administrative staff, I found that I would be better served learning how to create the website from scratch. Since then I have completed a full-stack coding bootcamp and re-created the communication hub with a team of five other developers. I have gained even more of a passion for software development whether it be to pave the road to efficiency with code, create digital art-forms via sleek and user friendly software applications, or create opportunities for all to experience and have access to the arts.
